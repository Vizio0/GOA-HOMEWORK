    Bikes (bicycles) are two-wheeled vehicles powered by pedaling. They’re used for transport, sport, fitness, and recreation. 🚲 Main types of bikes Road bikes – built for speed on smooth roads, lightweight and thin tires Mountain bikes (MTB) – strong frame, thick tires, used for rough terrain and off-road trails Hybrid bikes – mix of road + mountain bikes, good for everyday city riding BMX bikes – small, strong bikes used for tricks and stunts Electric bikes (e-bikes) – have a motor that helps you pedal easier and faster
